# Ring of Recoil Helper
A RuneLite plugin which helps with the regular usage of jewelry with recoiling effects in RuneScape.
Includes the following features:
- Proper recoil tracking based on latent hit splats applied to attackers
- Display how many charges are left on the currently equipped ring
- Notifications at popular places which make use of rings of recoil/ring of suffering:
  - Barbarian Assault
  - Cerberus
  - Corporeal Beast
  - Drakes
  - Duke Sucellus
  - God Wars Dungeon
  - Grotesque Guardians
  - Inferno
  - Kalphite Queen
  - Lithkren Vault
  - Mimic
  - Mithril dragons
  - Moons of Peril
  - Phantom Muspah
  - Sarachnis
  - Scorpia
  - Smoke Dungeon
  - TzHaar Fight Cave
  - Vardorvis
  - Yama
  - Zulrah
- Warning when wearing recoiling jewelry at an immune enemy
- Control which types of recoil notifications appear, and when they appear
- Display charges of recoiling rings stored within bank
- Play sound FX when your ring of recoil shatters
